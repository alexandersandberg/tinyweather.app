Simple forecasts

Tiny Weather makes staying up-to-date with the weather simple. If you’re looking for more advanced forecasts this app may not be for you.

Free without ads and subscriptions

Tiny Weather is 100% free without ads and subscriptions that you will forget to cancel.

Protecting your privacy

Tiny Weather does not sell your data like many other weather apps. We don’t even collect it.

Giving to those in need

50% of all donations to Tiny Weather are given to GiveWell’s Maximum Impact Fund to help people in need.
